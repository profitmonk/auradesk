SMS Messaging Program

Auradesk provides intelligent SMS messaging services to property managers and their residents for important property management communications.

What You'll Receive

  • Maintenance Updates: Real-time status updates on maintenance requests and work orders
  • Payment Reminders: Timely rent payment notifications and confirmations
  • Lease Notifications: Important updates about lease renewals and terms
  • Property Announcements: Community updates and important notices
  • Package Alerts: Notifications when deliveries arrive
  • Emergency Communications: Urgent alerts and critical updates

How to Opt-In

Residents provide consent to receive SMS messages during the lease signing process. Property managers obtain verbal consent from residents who wish to receive text message notifications and updates about their residence.

Message frequency varies depending on your account activity and property updates. You may receive multiple messages per week during active periods. Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier's plan.

How to Opt-Out

You can opt-out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation of your opt-out request. You can also contact your property manager directly to be removed from text message communications.

For help or questions about messages, reply HELP to any message or contact us using the information below.
